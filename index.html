About Me

I am a second year Ph.D. student here at the University of Florida. I am currently also pursuing a Ph.D. minor tract in Urban Planning from the Department of Urban and Regional Planning at the University of Florida. I received my Bachelor's and Master's degrees from Pulchowk Campus, Tribhuvan University, Nepal. I am currently being supervised for my doctoral studies by Dr. Sivaramakrishnan Srinivasan.

My research mainly focuses on:
a) understanding causal factors behind different kinds of crashes;
b) developing statistical and machine learning models to predict and analyze spatial and temporal factors behind crashes.
I am also interested in exploring different facets of Travel Behavior Studies and their implications in Transportation Planning.
